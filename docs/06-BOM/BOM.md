## Overview
Each student in this course has a $60 budget which is used to purchase the necessary components for their subsystem. Based on the block diagram, component selection, microcontroller, and power budget, I have created a bill of materials for the gyroscope subsystem, listing the vendors, part numbers, and datasheets. Some components are provided by the course or are being shared among the members of Team 201. These components are marked down as such and, though they are listed in the BOM below, do not need to be purchased.
