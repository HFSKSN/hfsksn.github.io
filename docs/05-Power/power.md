## Overview

In order to ensure that each component of the subsystem was appropriate for the module, a power budget was created which took each voltage and current draw from the major components and measured them against the input voltages and voltager regulator supply. This power budget information was also used to help our team choose an appropriate power supply for when all of our modules are connected, that being the 9V 3A battery. A USB and wall power supply was also included for individual module testing and programming.
